Frequently Asked Questions

What's the revenue split?

We work on a 50/50 partnership model. This ensures we're fully aligned - we only win when you win.

Do you work with new creators?

Yes! We work with creators at every level—whether you're just starting or already established. Our approach adapts to your current position.

How does the chat team work?

We deploy our trained chat team 24/7 on your account. They handle all DMs using proven sales frameworks that convert subscribers into high-paying fans.

What platforms do you focus on?

Our core traffic sources are Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube. These organic platforms provide the highest quality subscribers long-term.

How involved do I need to be?

You focus on creating content about 5 days per week. We handle editing, marketing, ideas, and direction—plus all chatting and operations—so you work less and earn more. Weekly strategy calls keep you updated.

What if I'm not comfortable showing my face?

While face-based content performs best, we can discuss alternatives during your strategy call. However, faceless accounts are very difficult to scale organically.
